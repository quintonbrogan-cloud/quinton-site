About

New England based, world-traveling DP with a thing for natural light (or the illusion of it). I love crafting images that feel honest—subtly beautiful, never distracting. Whether it's a run-and-gun doc or a meticulously lit commercial, my goal's the same: make it real, make it compelling, and make sure nobody notices the lights (even when they're everywhere).

I've shot on every kind of crew, in every kind of place—from Kenyan savannas to Costa Rican surf breaks. Along the way, I've been lucky to collaborate with brands like Deloitte, Red Bull, ESPN, Best Buy, and Mitsubishi—always chasing that sweet spot where technical precision meets gut-level storytelling.

(Also: I will not overuse the word "cinematic" in this bio.)
